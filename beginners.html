Getting Your License

Start by visiting your local DMV website for motorcycle licensing info. Requirements vary by state, but most include a written test, skills exam, and vision check.

Beginner Tips

Take it slow. Start in a parking lot or open area, wear full gear, and learn how your bike responds before hitting the roads.

Find an MSF Course

Ready to learn hands-on? The Motorcycle Safety Foundation offers beginner courses in every state. Find your local MSF course here!
